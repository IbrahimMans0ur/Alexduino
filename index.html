What is Alexduino?

Alexduino is the premier Arduino-based hackathon and project contest tailored for innovative students across Alexandria and Egypt. Organized by the IEEE SSCS Alexandria Chapter, we aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, teaching students more about electronics, programming, and making Alexandria a hub for creativity and innovation.

  • Open to all school students in Egypt.
  • Focus on hardware-software integration.
  • Exciting prizes and networking opportunities.
